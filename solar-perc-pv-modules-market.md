<p><p>The Solar PERC PV modules market is primarily segmented into Mono PERC and Poly PERC cells. Mono PERC cells are made from a single crystalline silicon structure, offering higher efficiency and better performance in low-light conditions, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. In contrast, Poly PERC cells are crafted from multiple silicon crystals, providing a more cost-effective solution with slightly lower efficiency but greater manufacturing scalability. Both types leverage the Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell technology to enhance energy conversion rates, catering to diverse market needs.</p></p>
