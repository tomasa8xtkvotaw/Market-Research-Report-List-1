<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas flow dryer market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I dryers utilize direct gas flow, efficiently transferring heat to materials through direct contact with heated gases, ideal for bulk drying applications. Type II dryers, on the other hand, employ indirect gas flow, heating the air without direct contact, making them suitable for more delicate materials that require gentle handling. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on product sensitivity and drying efficiency.</p></p>
