<p><p>The blood purification market consists of several types, including Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and other methods. CBP is designed for patients with acute kidney injury, offering continuous treatment and better hemodynamic stability. Hemodialysis, the most common form, uses a machine to filter waste from the blood periodically, typically for chronic kidney disease patients. Other market options encompass alternative therapies, including peritoneal dialysis and innovative filtration technologies, catering to various patient needs and conditions in blood purification.</p></p>
