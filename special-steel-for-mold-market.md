<p><p>The Special Steel for Mold market comprises three primary types: Hot Rolled Mold Steel, Cold Rolled Mold Steel, and Plastic Mold Steel. Hot Rolled Mold Steel is produced at high temperatures, offering excellent toughness and machinability, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Cold Rolled Mold Steel undergoes a process at lower temperatures, enhancing its precision and surface finish, making it ideal for intricate molds. Plastic Mold Steel is specifically designed for plastic injection molds, providing durability and resistance to wear and deformation.</p></p>
