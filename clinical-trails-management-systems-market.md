<p><p>Clinical Trials Management Systems (CTMS) can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based CTMS offers remote accessibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency, facilitating real-time data sharing and collaboration among stakeholders. Conversely, on-premise CTMS is installed locally on a company's servers, providing enhanced control over data security and compliance. While on-premise solutions may require higher upfront costs and maintenance, they are often preferred by organizations prioritizing data privacy and specific regulatory needs. Both types support efficient clinical trial management.</p></p>
