<p><ul><li>Passive Mixers</li><li>Active Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SHF RF mixers market is primarily categorized into two types: passive mixers and active mixers. Passive mixers rely on non-linear components, such as diodes, for signal mixing without requiring external power, offering simplicity and high linearity but limited gain. In contrast, active mixers utilize amplifying components, such as transistors, providing higher gain and improved performance in low-level signal applications. Both types serve crucial roles in signal processing across diverse applications in communication, radar, and electronic warfare systems.</p></p>
