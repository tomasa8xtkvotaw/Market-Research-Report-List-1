High Pressure Injectors are designed to deliver contrast media at elevated pressures, ensuring optimal imaging quality during CT scans, particularly for challenging vascular studies. Pressure Contrast Injectors, on the other hand, utilize controlled pressure settings to enhance the precision and safety of contrast media delivery, minimizing risks such as extravasation. Both types are essential for enhancing diagnostic imaging capabilities in medical practices.</p></p>
