<p><p>The heavy-duty casters market is divided into two primary types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters have a rotating wheel assembly, allowing for easy maneuverability and fluid movement in various directions, making them ideal for handling complex layouts. Rigid casters, on the other hand, have fixed wheels that move in a straight line, providing stability and load support for heavier loads. Both types are essential in industrial settings, contributing to operational efficiency and equipment mobility.</p></p>
