<p><p>The phototherapy treatment market comprises three main types: conventional, LED, and fiberoptic. Conventional phototherapy typically uses fluorescent lamps for skin conditions like psoriasis. LED phototherapy employs light-emitting diodes that provide targeted treatment with lower heat and energy consumption. Fiberoptic phototherapy utilizes fiberoptic cables to deliver light directly to affected areas, offering flexibility and accessibility in treatment. Each type has unique advantages, catering to varying clinical needs and patient preferences in managing skin and other health conditions.</p></p>
