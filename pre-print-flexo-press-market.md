<p><ul><li>Central Impression Flexo Press</li><li>Stack Flexo Press</li><li>Inline Flexo Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pre-print flexo press market encompasses three main types: Central Impression Flexo Press, Stack Flexo Press, and Inline Flexo Press. </p><p>Central Impression Flexo Press features a central cylinder that holds multiple ink stations, allowing for high-quality printing on large rolls. Stack Flexo Press consists of stacked printing units, enabling compact designs suitable for short runs. Inline Flexo Press integrates various processes, such as coating and laminating, in a single setup, streamlining production and enhancing efficiency for diverse packaging applications.</p></p>
