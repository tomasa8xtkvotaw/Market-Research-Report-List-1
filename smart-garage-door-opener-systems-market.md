<p><p>Smart garage door opener systems are categorized into several market types based on their mechanisms. Belt drive opener systems use a rubber belt for quiet operation and minimal maintenance, ideal for homes close to living areas. Chain drive opener systems employ a durable metal chain, offering robust performance but more noise, suitable for detached garages. Screw drive opener systems utilize a threaded steel rod for efficient lifting, requiring less maintenance. Other systems may include direct drive or smart-enabled variants, expanding functionality and convenience in home automation.</p></p>
